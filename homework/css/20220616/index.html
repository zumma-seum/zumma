Yoo Jeung Ok

We interviewed Mrs. Yoo Jeong-ok, who was transformed from an ordinary wife in a small church to an angel who touched the hearts of many people with one book and became famous as a writer. The interview was held on January 3, 2006 (Tuesday) at the Palgakjeong Pavilion in Bukaksan Mountain, Seoul, and was attended by Pastor Kim Su-cheol of the Street Mission, which is well known in the United States. Yu's wife started with a story about the book that made her famous, "I am grateful to be able to cry with someone who is crying (publishing a Christian book)". Mrs. Yu went to many publishers with the manuscript, but was rejected. The contents of this book are written on the homepage of the high school reunion of graduating girls.
